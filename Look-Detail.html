THE LOOK SUMMER EASE

Relaxed, effortless, and chic. Exactly how summer should be.

Your outlook is rosy with a quintessentially summer look that transitions easily from day to night. Enhance your natural glow in an understated, elegant, timelessly Trish way.

shop the look

bring this look to life

  • Step 1

    Liberally apply Eye Base Essentials to the lower eyelid. Use our Laydown Brush to blend swiftly up and out, from lash to brow, and to the inner corner of the eye.

  • step 2

    Sweep Lash Curling Mascara through top and bottom lashes for eye definition.

  • Step 3

    Smooth a dime-sized amount of Beauty Balm Instant Solutions® SPF 35 over skin, starting at the center of the face. Use our Perfect Foundation Brush, Professional Sponge, or your fingertips.

  • Step 4

    Place Liquid Face Color onto the high apples of your cheeks. Using the pad of your finger or a Trish Face Brush, pat the color gently into your skin until blended.

  • Step 5

    Complete the look with summer-perfect Veil Lip Color in Modern Rose.
